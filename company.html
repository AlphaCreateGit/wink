ABOUT WINK HOTEL

Visionary Hospitality
Experience Creators

Wink is rooted in modern traditions. The vision of Indochina Kajima, positioned to become Vietnam’s best boutique hotelier. Two pioneers joining forces to redefine affordable luxury in Vietnam and beyond.


What sets Wink apart is its contemporary ethos of chic + creative design, deep functionality and traditional Vietnamese values. Quá đỉnh!


Wink’s pillar include genuine and casual customer service and stellar guest experience. These standouts have wow-ed the market since the Wink hotel debuted in March 202 and became one of the best hotels in Saigon.
